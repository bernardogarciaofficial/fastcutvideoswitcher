Step 1: Upload Your Song

Select the main audio track (your song or backing track). This will be used for syncing and editing your music video.

Step 2: Video Takes

For each camera angle, upload a video or record a take. You can also download your latest take for each track.

Step 3: Live Edit Your Music Video

Use the camera switcher below to live-switch between tracks while the music plays. When you're ready, record your live edit, preview it, or export the final video!
Instructions:
1. Upload your main song file.
2. For each track, upload or record a take.
3. Use the camera switcher to select the active video.
4. Click "Record Full Edit" to capture your live camera switching.
5. Preview or export your final video.
Tip: You can download any take from each track for review or backup.
